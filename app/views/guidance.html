Common signs of ghostly presence

  • unexplained temperature drops (especially sudden chills)
  • whispering or faint crying where no one is present
  • objects moving or tilting on their own
  • flickering candles or lights with no electrical fault
  • a strong smell of old perfume, tobacco or damp air from nowhere

If you experience these signs, remain calm. Many hauntings are simply passing visitations or echoes of restless energy.
