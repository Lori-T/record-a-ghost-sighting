Expenses you can claim

You can claim reasonable lunch expenses when travelling for work or attending meetings away from your normal workplace.

Allowed items

  • sandwiches, wraps or salads
  • non-alcoholic drinks, such as water, tea, coffee or juice
  • meal deals up to £7
  • hot food from a café or supermarket as part of a working day

If you're not sure whether an item is claimable, check the full staff expenses policy or ask your manager.
