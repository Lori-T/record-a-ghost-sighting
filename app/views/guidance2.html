How to make a report

You can record your ghost sighting online, or send your report through secure psychic channels (or email will do).

You’ll need to provide

  • your full name and contact email
  • the type of ghost or activity you witnessed
  • the date, time and location of the incident
  • a short description of what happened
  • any photographic or spectral evidence (optional)

Once submitted, your report will be reviewed by an authorised investigator. Most hauntings are processed within 5 working nights.

If activity increases or becomes dangerous (for example, levitating furniture or spectral possession), please contact your local Emergency Ghost Response Unit.
