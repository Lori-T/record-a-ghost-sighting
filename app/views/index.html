Welcome to the Department for Supernatural Affairs prototype service. This haunted GOV.UK-style service has been intentionally built with accessibility issues to help you test, identify and understand common accessibility barriers — while keeping an eye out for ghosts. 👻

Each page hides at least one accessibility issue (and possibly a supernatural one too). Use assistive technology, zoom, or your keyboard to uncover the problems lurking in the shadows.
