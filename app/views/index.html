Record a ghost sighting – trainer information

Welcome to the Department for Supernatural Affairs training prototype. This intentionally haunted GOV.UK-style service is built to help participants find, test and understand accessibility issues hidden within a spooky user journey.

Each page contains one or more deliberate accessibility barriers (and perhaps a ghostly surprise 👻). Use this as a guided exercise to identify the issues, discuss their impact, and explore how to fix them.
