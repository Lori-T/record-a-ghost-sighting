Record a ghost sighting

Use this service to tell the Department for Supernatural Affairs about a ghost, spirit or haunting you’ve seen.

Your report helps us track supernatural activity and keep communities safe.

You can use this service to record:

  • a single ghost sighting
  • multiple hauntings in the same location

You’ll need to tell us:

  • where you saw the ghost
  • when you saw it
  • what it looked like (if you could tell)

Do not use this service to report other paranormal activity, such as vampires, werewolves or cursed pumpkins.
