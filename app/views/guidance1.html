Allowed and not allowed items

You can claim reasonable lunch expenses when travelling for work or attending meetings away from your normal workplace.

Not allowed

  • alcohol (including wine, beer or spirits)
  • snacks outside of a main meal, such as sweets or crisps
  • food or drink bought for someone else
  • claims made while working from home
  • takeaways or meals intended to be eaten at home

If you're not sure whether an item is claimable, check the full staff expenses policy or ask your manager.
